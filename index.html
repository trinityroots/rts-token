Tokenomics

Roots Token (RTS) is used solely as a utility token on the Roots ecosystem. Due to the following behaviors, the total supply is expected to fluctuate in accordance to rate of earn and usage. Tokens are earned through activities that contribute to the health of the Roots ecosystem. Through claiming, new tokens are minted into existence. Used tokens are removed from circulation through token burn, thus reducing total supply.

  • Name: Roots Token
  • Symbol: RTS
  • Type: ERC20
  • Expiration: none
  • Features: Mint, Burn, RBAC
  • Roles: Admin, Minter, Burner
  • Contract: View
  • Max Supply: 100,000,000
  • Decimals: 18
  • Blockchain: AVAX Fuji C-Chain

Claim

The claim system operates with the token through IERC20, the interface for ERC20. It holds both unclaimed and claimed balances. Unclaimed balances are set through calls from offchain and results in the minting of new tokens to increast the total supply.

Redeem

The redeem contract interfaces with RootsERC20 for the purpose of redeeming rewards. Through redeem, tokens are burned and the total supply is reduced.
